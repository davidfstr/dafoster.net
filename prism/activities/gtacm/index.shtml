GT ACM

I have been a member of the Georgia Tech Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) chapter since coming to Georgia Tech in 2005. Over time, I have become an active member in the organization.

Programming Team

In 2007 I was a member of Georgia Tech's premier team in the International Collegiate Programming Contest, the GT Yellow Jackets.

We won 2nd place in the 2007 Southeast Regional Programming Competition, out of 76 teams, and received an invitation to the World Finals in Banff, Alberta Canada.

Webmaster

In 2007, I took on the responsibility of maintaining the official GT ACM website. Since leaving Georgia Tech, others have been maintaining it in my stead.
